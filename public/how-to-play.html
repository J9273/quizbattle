Frequently Asked Questions

Can I join after the game starts?

No, once the first question has been answered, new teams cannot join. This keeps the game fair for everyone.

What if my device disconnects?

Just refresh the page and rejoin with the same team name. Your points and position will be saved!

Can one person be on multiple teams?

Technically yes, but it's more fun to have one team per person or group. Keep it fair!

How does Multiple Choice scoring work?

All teams who select the correct answer get points automatically. No need for the host to manually award points!

What happens at the end of the game?

When the host stops the episode, you'll see an animated podium celebration showing the top 3 winners and full rankings!
